# Privacy Policy for Upwork Cover Letter AI

**Effective Date:** December 26, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 26, 2025

## Introduction

Upwork Cover Letter AI ("the Extension", "we", "us", or "our") is a Chrome browser extension that helps Upwork freelancers generate AI-powered cover letters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Extension.

By using the Extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you use the Extension, you may provide the following information:

1. **OpenAI API Key**: Your personal OpenAI API key used to communicate with OpenAI's services
2. **Developer Profile Information**:
   - Full Name
   - Professional Title
   - Years of Experience
   - Professional Bio/Summary
   - Work Experience & Key Projects
   - Education & Certifications
   - Programming Languages
   - Frameworks & Technologies
   - Core Skills
   - Portfolio/GitHub URL
   - Hourly Rate
3. **Cover Letter Template**: Custom templates you create or modify
4. **Extension Preferences**: Settings such as AI model selection, auto-generate preferences, and job highlighting preferences

### Information Automatically Collected

The Extension automatically extracts information from Upwork job listings when you visit them, including:

- Job titles
- Job descriptions
- Required skills
- Budget information
- Client names (if publicly visible)
- Job posting times

**Important**: This information is extracted locally in your browser and is only used temporarily to generate cover letters.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Cover Letter Generation**: Your profile information and job data are sent to OpenAI's API to generate personalized cover letters
2. **Extension Functionality**: To provide core features such as job highlighting, auto-generation settings, and template customization
3. **API Communication**: Your OpenAI API key is used exclusively to authenticate requests to OpenAI's services

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only

All your information is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage mechanisms:

- **Chrome Storage Sync**: Your settings and profile information are synchronized across your Chrome browser instances using Chrome's secure sync feature
- **No External Servers**: We do **NOT** store, transmit, or have access to any of your data on our own servers
- **No Cloud Database**: We do not maintain any cloud databases or external storage systems

### Storage Duration

Your data remains in Chrome's local storage until you:
- Manually delete it through the extension settings
- Uninstall the Extension
- Clear your Chrome browsing data

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI API

The Extension uses OpenAI's API to generate cover letters. When you use this feature:

1. Your profile information and job data are sent directly from your browser to OpenAI's servers
2. This communication uses your personal OpenAI API key
3. OpenAI's use of this data is governed by [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy) and [OpenAI's Terms of Service](https://openai.com/policies/terms-of-use)
4. We do not control how OpenAI processes or stores this information

**Important**: Please review OpenAI's privacy policy to understand how they handle your data.

### Upwork

The Extension operates on Upwork's website (upwork.com) to extract publicly visible job information. We:
- Do NOT access any private information from your Upwork account
- Only read publicly posted job listings that are visible in your browser
- Do NOT have access to your Upwork credentials, messages, or private data
- Do NOT store communication with Upwork on any external servers

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT** share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties, except:

1. **OpenAI API**: Job data and your profile information are sent to OpenAI when you generate cover letters (as described above)
2. **Legal Requirements**: If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

1. **Local Storage**: All data is stored locally using Chrome's secure storage APIs
2. **No Transmission**: Your API key and profile data are never transmitted to our servers
3. **HTTPS**: All communications with OpenAI's API use encrypted HTTPS connections
4. **No Logging**: We do not log, track, or monitor your usage of the Extension

However, please note:
- No method of electronic storage is 100% secure
- You are responsible for keeping your OpenAI API key confidential
- Chrome's security mechanisms protect your locally stored data

## Browser Permissions

The Extension requests the following browser permissions:

1. **storage**: To save your settings, profile, and preferences locally
2. **activeTab**: To access the currently active Upwork job page you're viewing
3. **host_permissions (https://*.upwork.com/*)**: To extract job information and inject the cover letter generation interface on Upwork pages

These permissions are used **solely** for the Extension's core functionality and **NOT** for tracking or data collection beyond what's necessary for the features.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access and Update
- Access and modify your profile information anytime through the Extension's settings page
- Update or change your cover letter templates
- Modify extension preferences

### Delete Your Data
- Clear your profile information through the "Reset to Default" button in settings
- Remove your API key from the Extension at any time
- Uninstall the Extension to remove all stored data from your browser

### Opt-Out Options
- Disable auto-generation of cover letters
- Disable job highlighting features
- Use the Extension without providing optional profile information
